words
are birds
that arrive
with books
and spring
they love clouds the wind and trees some words are messengers that
come from far away from distant lands for them there are no
borders only stars moon and sun some words are familiar
like canaries others are exotic like the quetzal bird
some can stand the cold others migrate
with the sun to the south some words
die caged— they're difficult to
translate and others
build nests
have chicks
warm them
feed them
teach them how
to fly and one day
they go away in flocks
the letters on this page are
the prints they leave
